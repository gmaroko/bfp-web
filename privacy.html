Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Barefoot Power collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with our products, services, and partnerships.

  1. Home
  2. Privacy

Last updated: November 2, 2025

Information We Collect

When you interact with Barefoot Power — whether as a customer, partner, or visitor — we collect limited information to provide our services responsibly.

Personal Information

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Organization details for partnership inquiries
  • Purchase and service history
  • Feedback or support requests

Usage Data

We may collect non-identifiable usage data such as website activity, product interest, and engagement metrics to improve our offerings.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to deliver solar solutions, support customers, and strengthen partnerships.

Primary Uses

  1. Responding to inquiries and partnership requests
  2. Providing product support and after-sales services
  3. Improving product design and customer experience
  4. Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or trade your personal information. Data is only shared with trusted service providers or partners when necessary to deliver our solutions.

We Never Sell Your Data

Your trust is central to our mission. Any data shared is strictly for service delivery and compliance purposes.

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Encryption

Personal data is encrypted during transmission and storage to ensure confidentiality.

Secure Storage

Data is stored securely with restricted access and regular monitoring.

Your Rights

You have rights regarding your personal information, including access, correction, and deletion.

Right to Access

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

You may correct inaccurate or incomplete information at any time.

Right to Erasure

You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to improve website functionality, analyze usage, and enhance user experience.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

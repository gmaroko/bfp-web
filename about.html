Our Origin Story

Barefoot Power was founded in 2005 in Sydney, Australia, with a vision to provide affordable solar lighting and energy solutions to underserved communities. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a global leader in sustainable living and climate action.

Global Reach

Over the years, Barefoot Power has built in-country subsidiaries in Africa and India, and established strong partnerships across Asia-Pacific and South America. Our products and programs are trusted worldwide for their durability, affordability, and impact.

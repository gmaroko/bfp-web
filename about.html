Delivering Solar Solutions Since 2005

Barefoot Power specializes in high-quality solar lighting and energy systems designed for large-scale distribution, institutional programs, and custom product development. Our solutions support global partners working in off-grid and underserved regions.

A Trusted Partner for Scalable Solar Deployment

With nearly two decades of experience, Barefoot Power has supplied millions of products to NGOs, institutional buyers, and distributors implementing solar programs across Africa, Asia-Pacific, and the Americas.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, affordable solar energy solutions that empower communities and support large-scale development programs worldwide.

Our Vision

Our Commitment

Our Origin

Founded in 2005 in Sydney, Australia, Barefoot Power pioneered affordable solar lighting solutions for underserved markets. Today, we operate across multiple continents with a focus on large-scale institutional solar deployments.

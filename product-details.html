Barefoot Connect

Reliable Solar Home Systems for Every Step of the Energy Ladder

Barefoot Connect serves single/double rooms with extensibility to four+ rooms, security lighting, and plug-ins. It supports a growing customer journey from basic lighting to sustainable family lifestyle and income generation.

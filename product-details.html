Solar Home System

Barefoot Connect

Scalable Solar Home System for Energy Access Programs & Distribution Partners

Barefoot Connect is a modular Solar Home System (SHS) designed for wide-scale distribution and deployment through partners including distributors, NGOs, SACCOs, MFIs, and government electrification programs.

The system supports lighting, phone charging, and a growing range of appliances, enabling progressive energy access — from single-room lighting to multi-room setups and productive use devices.

Key Features:

  • Modular & scalable system design
  • Compatible with TVs, radios, fans, fridges, and CCTV
  • Long-life battery and high-efficiency solar panel
  • Multiple lighting points and security lighting
  • PAYGo-ready versions available on request
  • Custom configurations available for partner programs

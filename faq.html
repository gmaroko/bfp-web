Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Barefoot Power’s solar products, partnerships, support, and global impact.

  1. Home
  2. FAQ

What types of solar solutions does Barefoot Power provide?

We offer a full range of products from portable solar lanterns (Barefoot GO) to scalable solar home systems (Barefoot Connect) and solar-powered appliances such as TVs, fridges, and fans.

Can Barefoot Connect power household appliances?

Yes. Barefoot Connect supports appliances including TVs, radios, fans, fridges, and CCTV systems, making it ideal for families and small businesses.

How do I become a distribution partner?

You can reach out via our Contact page and select “Distribution Partnership Inquiry” in the form. Our team will guide you through onboarding and training.

Does Barefoot Power provide installation and after-sales support?

Yes. We provide installation services, user training in maintenance, and ongoing after-sales support to ensure long-term product reliability and customer satisfaction.

Where is Barefoot Power located globally?

Our Africa HQ is in Nairobi, Kenya. We also have a manufacturing base in China and partnerships across Asia-Pacific and South America.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our solar products, partner programs, technical support, and global availability.

  1. Home
  2. FAQ

What types of products does Barefoot Power offer?

We provide portable solar lanterns (Barefoot GO), scalable solar home systems (Barefoot Connect), and a range of solar-compatible appliances including televisions, radios, fans, and refrigerators. All products can be customized for NGO, government, and distributor programs.

Do you offer custom product development for partners?

Yes. We support custom configurations, co-branded products, kit adjustments, and program-specific solutions for distributors, NGOs, and institutional partners. Contact us to discuss requirements.

What are the minimum order quantities (MOQ) for bulk purchases?

MOQs vary by product type and customization level. Standard orders begin at pallet-level quantities, while fully customized products may require higher MOQs. Email us for detailed MOQ guidelines.

Does Barefoot Power provide warranty and after-sales support?

All products come with a manufacturer warranty. We also support partners with technician training, spare parts supply, troubleshooting documentation, and extended warranty programs on request.

Can Barefoot Connect power appliances?

Yes. Barefoot Connect supports TVs, radios, fans, USB devices, and even fridges depending on the configuration. Appliance compatibility lists and technical specifications are available upon request.

How do I become a distributor or partner?

You can reach us via the Contact page or through email at info@barefootpower.com. Our team will provide onboarding details, product catalogues, training information, and commercial requirements.

Are technical manuals and product specifications available?

Yes. We provide product datasheets, installation manuals, user guides, and certification documents. These can be shared upon request depending on partner needs.

What regions does Barefoot Power serve?

We operate across Africa, Asia-Pacific, and South America through distributors, NGOs, and program partners. Our Africa HQ is located in Nairobi, Kenya, with a manufacturing base in China.

Reach & Scale

We have provided clean, modern lighting and energy services to over 2,000,000 households, impacting the lives of more than 10 million people.

People Reached

8.7 million people now benefit from clean, modern solar power systems.

Household Savings

Families have saved over US$414 million on kerosene, batteries, and phone charging.

Study & Productivity

2,000 million extra hours gained for child study and productive activities at night.

Climate Action

2.1 million tonnes of CO2 emissions averted through clean energy adoption.

Health

4.2 million people experiencing better health outcomes from reduced indoor air pollution.

Economic Inclusion

Over 3,000 micro-entrepreneurs, SMEs, and employees gained income and solar industry experience.




Transformation

Lives are transformed by eliminating indoor air pollution and reducing energy costs associated with off-grid living. Our impact extends to improved education standards, healthier families, and expanded socio-economic inclusion across communities.

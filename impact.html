Partners

Delivering Scalable Social & Environmental Impact

Barefoot Power supports households, institutions, NGOs, and government programs with clean, reliable solar energy. Our solutions drive measurable outcomes in education, health, economic growth, and climate action.

  1. Home
  2. Impact

Reach & Scale

Barefoot Power’s products have reached over 2 million households, improving life for more than 10 million people across Africa and Asia-Pacific.

People Reached

More than 8.7 million people currently rely on Barefoot Power systems for clean, affordable lighting and energy.

Household Savings

Over US$414 million saved collectively from reduced kerosene, batteries, and phone charging expenses.

Study & Productivity

More than 2 billion hours unlocked for studying and productivity through extended, clean nighttime lighting.

Climate Impact

An estimated 2.1 million tonnes of CO2 emissions avoided by replacing polluting fuels with solar energy.

Health Benefits

Cleaner indoor air for 4.2 million people, reducing respiratory issues associated with kerosene lighting.

Economic Inclusion

Over 3,000 micro-entrepreneurs and technicians supported through distribution, servicing, and retail networks.

Where We Work

Our energy access programs operate across diverse geographies — from rural communities to peri-urban centers — in partnership with NGOs, governments, distributors, and community groups.

Africa

Large-scale deployment across East, West, and Southern Africa powered by NGOs, distributors, and national programs.

Asia-Pacific

Community livelihood, education, and disaster-relief projects supported across the region.

Emergency & Relief Efforts

Lighting and energy solutions provided for humanitarian response, refugee camps, and disaster-affected areas.

How We Measure Impact

We work with partners to ensure every deployment produces measurable, verifiable outcomes aligned with SDGs, particularly SDG 7 (Affordable & Clean Energy).

Field Data & Surveys

We collect usage, satisfaction, and impact data directly from households and program beneficiaries.

Partner Reporting

NGO, institutional, and government partners share program outcomes and verified results.

Environmental Metrics

Energy savings, carbon avoidance, and product lifespan performance are tracked to global standards.

Transforming Communities

From improved education and safer homes to increased business productivity, Barefoot Power’s solutions are enabling cleaner, healthier, and more inclusive communities. Our impact is amplified through our partners, who play a central role in scaling solar access to households and institutions worldwide.
